Automated Meter Reading (AMR)

Is the technology of automatically collecting consumption, diagnostic, and status data from energy metering devices transferring that data to a central database for billing, troubleshooting, and analyzing data. This timely information coupled with analysis can help both utility providers and customers better control the use and production of electric energy, gas usage, or water consumption.
