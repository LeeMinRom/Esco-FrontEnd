Advance Metering Infrastructure (AMI)

Is the latest version offering one solutions that combines the functionalities of automatic meter reading (AMR) automatic meter management (AMM), metering data management (MDM), business intelligence (BI) and more.

AMI enables two-way communication meters reading electrical consumption at a higher frequency. This information can be processed in real-time and signals sent to manage demand. Demand-side management requires a lot of data processing to understand the load patterns and to design proper signals that enable optimal use of the distribution grid and manage reserve and frequency response. Collecting data at each meter is critical for energy suppliers and distribution system operators to better understand the patterns of electrical energy users so they can elaborate market signals and understand customer behavior.

It performs three operations: initially, it allows services to measure real-time, detailed information. Second, it collects all the data from various sources. Lastly, it transmits data to the specific device in the network (Mohassel et al., 2014). Utilizing AMI services ensures the consumers with advanced services and products for minimized quality of services for each region based on their country regulations. Consequently, productivity can be increased so that empowerment can be expanded for the involvement of stakeholders in the electricity domain.
